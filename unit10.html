Unit 10

Likes & Free Time

By the end you can: say what you like and dislike, talk about hobbies, seasons and weather. Exam skill: "M'agrada…" sentences are expected in both the writing and oral papers.

The verb agradar /əɣɾəˈða/ (to like)

It works "backwards": the thing liked is the grammatical subject. Use the indirect pronouns em, et, li, ens, us, els before it.

CatalanIPAEnglish
M'agrada el cafè./məˈɣɾaðə əl kəˈfɛ/I like coffee. (singular thing → agrada)
M'agraden els llibres./məˈɣɾaðən əls ˈʎiβɾəs/I like books. (plural thing → agraden)
T'agrada llegir?/təˈɣɾaðə ʎəˈʒi/Do you like reading? (verb → agrada)
Li agrada la música./li əˈɣɾaðə lə ˈmuzikə/He/She likes music.
No ens agrada gens./ˌnɔ ənz əˈɣɾaðə ˈʒens/We don't like it at all.
M'encanta!/məŋˈkantə/I love it!

Weather with fer

Catalan "does" the weather: Quin temps fa? /ˌkin ˈtems ˈfa/ What's the weather like? — Fa sol / fred / calor / vent. /fə ˈsɔl ˈfɾɛt kəˈlo ˈben/ It's sunny / cold / hot / windy. But: Plou. /ˈplɔw/ It's raining. · Neva. /ˈnɛβə/ It's snowing. · Està ennuvolat. /əsˈta ənnuβuˈlat/ It's cloudy.

Phrases
Què t'agrada fer?/ˌkɛ təˈɣɾaðə ˈfe/What do you like doing?
M'agrada molt cuinar./məˈɣɾaðə ˌmol kujˈna/I really like cooking.
No m'agrada gens el futbol./ˌnɔ məˈɣɾaðə ˈʒens əl fubˈbɔl/I don't like football at all.
Quin temps fa avui?/ˌkin ˈtems ˈfa əˈβuj/What's the weather like today?
A l'estiu fa molta calor./ə ləsˈtiw fə ˌmoltə kəˈlo/In summer it's very hot.

Laia: Què t'agrada fer el cap de setmana?

/ˌkɛ təˈɣɾaðə ˈfe əl ˌkab də səmˈmanə/ — What do you like doing at the weekend?

Marc: M'agrada llegir i córrer. I a tu?

/məˈɣɾaðə ʎəˈʒi i ˈkorə | i ə ˈtu/ — I like reading and running. And you?

Laia: A mi m'encanta cuinar. Però no m'agraden els esports.

/ə ˈmi məŋˈkantə kujˈna | pəˈɾɔ ˌnɔ məˈɣɾaðən əlz əsˈpɔrts/ — I love cooking. But I don't like sports.

Marc: Quin temps fa? Podem passejar?

/ˌkin ˈtems ˈfa | puˈðɛm pəsəˈʒa/ — What's the weather like? Can we go for a stroll?

Laia: Fa sol, però fa una mica de fred.

/fə ˈsɔl | pəˈɾɔ fə ˌunə ˌmikə ðə ˈfɾɛt/ — It's sunny, but a bit cold.

Exercises

EX 10.1

agrada or agraden?

  1. M' la música.
  2. M' els gats.
  3. T' cuinar?
  4. No ens les pel·lícules de por.
  5. Li viatjar.
EX 10.2

Add the right pronoun (em/et/li/ens/els).

  1. A mi agrada el te.
  2. A tu agrada córrer?
  3. A ella agraden els llibres.
  4. A nosaltres agrada passejar.
EX 10.3

Translate.

  1. I like reading.
  2. She likes apples.
  3. We love travelling.
  4. They don't like fish.
Model answer
1) M'agrada llegir. 2) Li agraden les pomes. 3) Ens encanta viatjar. 4) No els agrada el peix.
EX 10.4

Describe the weather in Catalan.

  1. It's hot and sunny.
  2. It's raining and windy.
  3. It's cold and cloudy.
  4. In winter it snows.
Model answer
1) Fa calor i fa sol. 2) Plou i fa vent. 3) Fa fred i està ennuvolat. 4) A l'hivern neva.
EX 10.5

Find and fix the mistake.

  1. M'agraden el cafè.
  2. M'agrada els gats.
  3. Fa plou avui.
Model answer
1) M'agrada el cafè. 2) M'agraden els gats. 3) Plou avui.
EX 10.6

Read the IPA, write the word.

  1. /kujˈna/
  2. /iˈβɛrn/
  3. /ˈpluʒə/
EX 10.7

Exam-style writing: three sentences — two things you like (one plural!), one you dislike, and today's weather.

Model answer
Sample: M'agrada molt llegir. M'agraden els gats. No m'agrada gens el futbol. Avui fa sol però fa fred.
