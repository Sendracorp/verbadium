# Pricing — Verbadium

Verbadium sells each language course as a one-time purchase. No subscription, no recurring fees.

## Catalan A1
- Price: €25.00 EUR, one-time
- Access: Lifetime, including future updates
- Includes: 12 units, 108 interactive exercises, a full timed mock A1 exam, a 275-entry glossary with native-speaker audio, and progress sync across devices
- Free preview: Unit 1 (no account needed)
- Taught in: English, Spanish, French, Russian, German — each sold separately at €25
- Checkout: card payment via Paddle (merchant of record; tax added at checkout based on buyer location)

## Catalan A2
- Price: €50.00 EUR, one-time
- Access: Lifetime, including future updates
- Includes: 15 units (past tenses, weak & combined pronouns, future and conditional), 142 interactive exercises, a full timed mock A2 exam, a glossary with native-speaker audio, and progress sync across devices
- Free preview: Unit 1 (no account needed)
- Taught in: English, Spanish, French, Russian, German — each sold separately at €50
- Checkout: card payment via Paddle (merchant of record; tax added at checkout based on buyer location)

## Notes
- Each course and each teaching language is a separate purchase (A1 €25, A2 €50); buying one does not include the others.
- Tax is added at checkout based on your location (Paddle is the merchant of record).
